Who we are

Where disciplined thinking meets practical execution

Bohun Defence Advisory is a boutique firm helping defence and dual-use teams prepare for investment, international programmes, and strategic partnerships.

We work at the intersection of venture logic, defence requirements, and export-control awareness — providing founders with clarity, structure, and access.

Defence innovation matters — but it becomes meaningful only when it is structurable, understandable, and fundable.
